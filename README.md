# 📊 James Superstore Sales & Customer Insights (Excel + Python + Power BI | EDA & BI Project)

---

## 🚀 Overview

This project is an end-to-end **data analytics system** designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, profitability, and operational efficiency of a retail superstore.

It provides actionable insights into **revenue trends, customer satisfaction, product performance, returns, and regional sales patterns** to support data-driven decision-making.

The project combines **Excel (data preprocessing), Python (EDA & analysis), and Power BI (interactive dashboards)** to deliver a complete business intelligence solution.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales, profit, and customer behavior
* Identify seasonal and regional sales trends
* Evaluate product category performance
* Understand return rate and complaint patterns
* Improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Retail businesses face challenges in managing **high return rates, declining sales in certain months, and low customer satisfaction scores**. This project aims to analyze historical transaction data to identify key issues affecting profitability and customer experience.

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes:

* Order details (Order ID, Order Date, Month, Order Status, Order Type)
* Customer details (Customer ID, Segment, Region, City)
* Product details (Category, Product Name, Quantity)
* Financial metrics (Sales, Cost, Profit, Discount, Profit %)
* Operational metrics (Shipping Method, Payment Method, Return Flag)
* Customer feedback (Ratings, Complaints Logged)

## 📊 Python EDA Insights

* Monthly sales show strong peaks in mid-year (seasonal demand)
* Clothing is the top-performing category
* Furniture is underperforming
* Digital payments dominate over cash
* Return rate is extremely high (~50%)
* Sales are distributed across multiple cities (no single dominance)

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Sales decline in last months indicates weak seasonal planning
* High return rate (~50%) is a critical business issue
* Clothing and electronics drive most revenue
* Customer satisfaction is relatively low (rating ~2.99)
* Balanced regional demand across major cities
* Strong dependency on mid-year sales cycles

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Reduce return rate through better quality control and delivery improvements
* Improve customer satisfaction via support and feedback systems
* Focus marketing on high-performing months (May–September)
* Strengthen furniture category with promotions and bundles
* Improve product descriptions to reduce returns
* Leverage digital payments for better customer experience
