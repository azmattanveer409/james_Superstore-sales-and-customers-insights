# James Superstore Sales & Customer Insights 

## Objective
Analyze sales and customer data to provide actionable insights for revenue growth, customer satisfaction, and order optimization.

## Dataset
26 columns including: Order ID, Order Date, Month, Region, City, Customer ID, Product Category, Product Name, Order Status, Order Type, Payment Method, Shipping Method, Return Flag, Complaint Logged, Feedback Rating, Customer Segment, Quantity, Cost per Unit, Total Cost, Unit Price, Discount, Total Sales, Profit, Profit Percentage, Net Unit Price, Channel.

## Tools Used
- Excel (Data cleaning, analysis, dashboard)
- Python (Data processing, visualization, insights)
- Power BI (Interactive dashboard)

## Process
1. Cleaned and preprocessed raw data in Excel.
2. Conducted exploratory data analysis and visualizations in Python.
3. Generated actionable insights and recommendations for sales, profit, and customer behavior.
4. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with KPIs and slicers.

## Power BI Dashboard Highlights
**KPIs:**  
- Total Sales: 16M  
- Total Profit: 4M  
- Profit Margin: 22.43%  
- Total Orders: 6,870  
- Return Orders: 3.39K  
- Average Rating: 2.99  

**Slicers:** Region (East, North, South, West), Segment (Offline, Online)  

**Charts:**  
1. Monthly Sales Trend – declining last two months  
2. Sales by Category – nearly equal  
3. Customer Complaint Rate – 49.72% complaints  
4. Sales by Top 10 Product Categories – roughly equal  
5. Order Status – Completed: 2.28K, Pending: 2.3K, Returned: 2.3K  
6. Yearly Sales by City – 2022 strong, 2023 slight decline, 2024 decline

## Key Insights & Recommendations
- Identify declining sales months for targeted marketing.  
- Monitor top product categories for inventory planning.  
- Reduce customer complaints with improved shipping and support.
